Who are we?

Sankalp is a group of people who are under Sushiksha, which is a Mentoring program organized For scholars of Vishwa Konkani Student Scholarship Fund (VKSSF) by members of VKSSF Alumni Association (VAA). Sushiksha was started in 2018 by Royal Denzil Sequeira, a member of VAA since 2015, with 20 mentees. Since then Sushiksha has grown to be family of 170 mentees and 20 mentors. Mentees of Sushiksha are undergraduate scholars of VKSSF who are determined to thrive and be successful in their area of study and career. Mentors of Sushiksha Program are experienced professionals who were once the scholars of VKSSF and have lived through their own share of successes and failures but believe in empowering the scholars early on in their career path. This website is a solution built for the problems encountered by students during the pandemic where , "WE" were supposed to look up several website for our academic details. We at Sankalp, have brought E-छात्र for your service, where you can get all services at one place.
